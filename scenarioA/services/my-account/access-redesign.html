My Service
Canada Account

Easily manage and view your Employment Insurance (EI), Canada Pension Plan (CPP) and Old Age Security (OAS) benefit details.

Use the sign-in option you registered with:

  Sign in with your GCKey   Use your online banking (Sign-in Partner)   Sign in with your province
Not sure which sign-in to use?

You need to sign in with the same username and password every time you use My Service Canada Account.

  • When you first registered for a My CRA Account, you selected a particular sign-in method.
  • You might have created a new CRA user id and password just for this account, and written it down or saved it in a Password manager.
  • Or when you registered, you may have decided to use your existing financial institution username and password rather than create a new set for Service Canada.
  • The CRA My Account tied that particular sign-in username and password with your tax and benefit information. Use that same method every time you sign in.

If you haven't already registered for a My Service Canada Account, register now.

Learn more about My Service Canada Account privacy and security

With My Service Canada Account you can:

  • check your EI claim, application status and messages
  • view your tax slips and records of payment for EI, CPP, OAS
  • apply for Canada Pension Plan (CPP)
  • change your address
  • set up/change direct deposit
  • receive email notifications of changes
  • and much more

My Service Canada Account is fast - transactions are processed immediately.

To avoid technical problems in MSCA:
Don’t bookmark secure MSCA pages (the pages you see after you log in), use a supported browser and enable cookies and JavaScript
